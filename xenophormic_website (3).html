Our Venture

Cosmodrop

Revolutionary autonomous drone delivery platform transforming last-mile logistics through cutting-edge AI and precision navigation systems.

The Challenge

Traditional last-mile delivery faces critical bottlenecks: traffic congestion increasing delivery times by 40%, rising fuel costs impacting profitability, limited delivery radius for ultra-fast commerce, and inconsistent delivery speeds during peak hours.

Market Opportunity

India's quick-commerce market is projected to reach $5.5 billion by 2025, growing at 15% annually. The drone logistics market in India is expected to hit $3.6 billion by 2030, with last-mile delivery representing the largest segment at 40%.

Our Solution

Cosmodrop bypasses ground traffic entirely, reducing 30-minute deliveries to 8-12 minutes. Our B2B model integrates seamlessly with existing delivery platforms, offering premium delivery options without infrastructure overhaul.
